Senior Thesis Project, MAE 440

An Electric Go-Kart Using Machine Learning Models For Autonomous Driving

Spring 2025 David Herrera
next

This thesis presents the design and implementation of a fully autonomous, electric go-kart, developed from a refurbished Facebook Marketplace frame. The primary objective was to demonstrate vision-based autonomous navigation using low-cost hardware and open-source software. The final system reliably performed lane following and object recognition (specifically for pedestrians and stop signs), validated through over 20 hours of autonomous testing on campus roads.
