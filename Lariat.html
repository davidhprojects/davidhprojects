2014 Ford F-150 Lariat Restoration

Complete engine swap and mechanical restoration of a modern Ford pickup.

Summer 2025 David Herrera

In June 2025, I purchased this 2014 Ford F-150 Lariat from Facebook Marketplace for $4,000. The listing mentioned engine trouble, but the truck’s clean body and well-kept interior made it a worthwhile project. After towing it home with another Ford Expedition, I began diagnosing the issue and found the original engine had overheated beyond repair.

A replacement engine was sourced from a local junkyard, and I completed a full engine swap along with several smaller repairs and upgrades. The project gave me valuable experience working on Ford’s modern powertrain systems, wiring harnesses, and cooling components.

The new engine cost $3500, and another estimated $1500 in parts was put in for a total cost of $9000

The truck is still with us today and currently worth around $12,000 — a rewarding turnaround for a summer’s worth of work and problem-solving.
