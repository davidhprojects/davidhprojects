2006 Range Rover HSE Restoration

Full mechanical and interior restoration of a luxury British SUV, featuring custom modifications and system rebuilds.

Summer 2021 David Herrera

This project showcases the restoration of a 2006 Range Rover HSE — a luxury SUV powered by a V8 engine. The goal was to bring the vehicle back to life while learning about the mechanical and electrical systems that define British automotive design.

Over the course of several months, I completed extensive repairs and custom upgrades across multiple systems — from suspension to interior — ultimately gaining a deep appreciation for how English engineering prioritizes luxury and comfort over maintainability.

Below are photos highlighting each stage of the project and the key work done along the way.

Air Suspension Overhaul

  • Replaced air struts and compressor unit
  • Fixed leaking air lines and recalibrated height sensors
  • Restored the vehicle’s signature smooth ride

Full Interior Restoration

  • Installed new leather seats, door panels, and carpeting
  • Refinished trim and restored seat electronics
  • Rebuilt the cabin to original luxury standards

Power Steering System Repair

  • Replaced pump and high-pressure lines
  • Eliminated fluid leaks and steering noise
  • Improved overall steering feel and responsiveness

A/C Compressor Replacement

  • Installed a new compressor and refrigerant lines
  • Vacuumed and recharged A/C system
  • Restored cold air output for cabin comfort

Custom Starlight Headliner

  • Installed fiber-optic starlight ceiling (Rolls-Royce style)
  • Wired into factory harness for OEM integration
  • Created a unique luxury interior ambiance

Final Result & Reflection

  • All major systems restored and upgrades completed
  • Extensive time investment, but rewarding experience
  • Takeaway: English vehicles prioritize comfort over serviceability
