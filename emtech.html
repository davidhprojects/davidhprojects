Emtech Summer Internship 2024: Athens, Greece

This post highlights my summer interning at Emtech P.C. in Athens, Greece

June - July 2024 David Herrera

Summer of 2024, I completed an internship at Emtech in Athens, Greece through Princeton's International Internship Program. Emtech is a Space Contractor for the European Space Agency (ESA) and is currently working on the Hellenic SpaceDawn Mission (HSD). My role was to focus on the Ground Segment - or the portion of the satellite mission relating to ground station satellite command. Specifically, my job was to research and validate the CubeSat Mission Control Software (EASE-Rise), along with discovering and validating the European Ground System Common core (EGS-CC).

The EGS-CC is a new open-source platform for the creation of mission control software for satellite missions. It is meant to reduce costs, as well as increase long term maintainability.

Ease-Rise is a new proprietary mission control software being developed by Telespazio. Emtech purchased this software to use for the HSD mission, and I was tasked with the initial implementation of the program.
